Hypothesis testing
The t-test high jump
See how degrees of freedom change the t-critical value. The jumper is your t-statistic, the bar is t-critical — can you clear it?
The F-test high jump
Same high jump concept for ANOVA. Adjust the number of groups and total participants to see how F-critical changes.
Confidence intervals
The golden egg hunt
Cast a net (your 95% CI) across a football field to find the golden egg (the population mean). Bigger samples = narrower net.
Does the CI include zero?
Adjust the mean difference, standard error, and sample size. Watch the CI change color as it crosses or avoids zero — and see the p-value flip.
CI overlap between two groups
Overlapping group CIs don't always mean "not significant." Compare individual CIs to the CI of the difference and see why.
Effect size and clinical significance
The effect size overlap machine
What does Cohen's d actually look like? Slide to see how much two groups of patients overlap at different effect sizes — from negligible to large.
The four possibilities
Toggle statistical significance, effect size, and the MCID on and off. Watch which quadrant lights up — and learn which combination is most dangerous for patient care.
