Unit 5 Class Activity

Interpreting Epidemiological Studies

A guided walkthrough of RR, ARR, NNT, and OR using interactive tools

How this activity works Work through the parts in order. You'll start with a mock journal article and a confidence check, then learn each of the four core epidemiology statistics one at a time, then apply all four to a new article. At the end, you'll re-rate your confidence and download a personalized completion certificate to upload to Canvas.
Best on a laptop or tablet. The interactive tools use sliders, tables, and side-by-side panels that work best on a larger screen. If you must use a phone, rotate to landscape mode.
