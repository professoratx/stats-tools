The effect size overlap machine

What does Cohen's d actually look like? Slide to see how much two groups of patients overlap.

How to use: Drag the slider to change Cohen's d — the effect size in standard deviation units. Watch the treatment group (pink) separate from the control group (blue). The overlap percentage tells you how much the two groups still share in common. Use the preset buttons to jump to Cohen's benchmarks: small (0.20), medium (0.51 — our mock article), and large (0.80).
